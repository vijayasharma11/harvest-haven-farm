Welcome to Harvest Haven Farm– Your Local Farm Attraction!

Nestled between Edinburgh and Carlisle, Harvest Haven Farm is a hidden gem for families, nature lovers, and anyone looking for a relaxing and enjoyable day out. Originally a working farm, we’ve transformed this beautiful space into a vibrant visitor attraction that celebrates the very best of local produce, wildlife, and outdoor fun.

Our Story

At Harvest Haven Farm, we believe in connecting people to nature in a meaningful way. What started as a small, family-run farm has grown into a place where visitors of all ages can enjoy a variety of activities—from meeting our friendly farm animals in the petting zoo to exploring the serene walking trails through our gardens. Whether you’re here to enjoy the peace and quiet of nature or to pick up fresh, locally grown produce, there’s something for everyone.

We are passionate about sustainability, community, and offering an experience that’s fun, educational, and family-friendly. Our mission is to share the beauty of the countryside, support local farmers and artisans, and create lasting memories for every visitor.

What We Offer

  • Petting Zoo: Meet our adorable animals, including goats, chickens, rabbits, and more. Our petting zoo is perfect for little ones looking to get up close with friendly farm animals.
  • Café & Farm Shop: Enjoy delicious homemade treats at our café and browse our shop for the best of local produce, plants, and unique gifts.
  • Play Park: Let the kids burn off some energy in our well-equipped play park with plenty of space to run, jump, and climb.
  • Gardens & Walks: Take a leisurely stroll through our lovely gardens or explore the beautiful walking trails around the farm, perfect for relaxing and enjoying the countryside.

A Place for Everyone

At Harvest Haven Farm, we’re not just a place to visit; we’re a community. From the families who come for a fun day out to the garden enthusiasts picking up seasonal plants, we welcome people from all walks of life. We pride ourselves on creating a space where both locals and tourists can come together, relax, and enjoy all that the farm has to offer.

Whether you’re stopping by for a quick break, enjoying a full day out, or exploring the countryside, we hope you’ll make Harvest Haven Farm a part of your journey. We look forward to welcoming you soon!

Meet the Team

Our dedicated team of farm staff, café servers, and gardeners are here to make your visit as enjoyable as possible. From helping you pick out the perfect plant to giving you tips on where to find the best views on our walks, we’re always happy to share our knowledge and make your experience memorable.

Sustainability at Harvest Haven Farm

We’re committed to protecting the environment and supporting sustainable farming practices. Our produce is grown with care, our gardens are maintained with organic methods, and we strive to reduce our environmental impact in all aspects of the farm’s operations. Every visit you make helps support our mission to preserve this beautiful corner of the countryside for future generations.

Visit Us

Located just off the main route between Edinburgh and Carlisle, we’re the perfect stop for a relaxing break on your journey. Our friendly staff are ready to greet you, and there’s always something new to discover, no matter the season.
